IEEE Young Professionals Summit 2025

Towards Sustainable Artificial Intelligence

About the Summit

The IEEE Young Professionals (YP) Summit is an annual gathering of IEEE YP leaders focused on making an impact in our communities and beyond. This year's theme is Towards Sustainable Artificial Intelligence.

Over three days, you'll engage in leadership training, career development, and discussions on AI-driven solutions and global challenges, while building lifelong connections with fellow young professionals.

Event Details

  • Who: 60-80 Young Professionals from IEEE Regions 1 and 2, and global YP leaders
  • When: August 22-24, 2025
  • Where: 60 Pine Street, New York City (Friday evening through Saturday evening). Sunday morning tours.
  • Hotel Tip: We recommend staying in New Jersey (affordable, accessible via PATH train).

Summit Highlights

  • Make an impact on Sustainable AI
  • Leverage the IEEE to advance your career
  • IEEE YP Leadership Training — learn to be a great YP Chair
  • Networking with peers and global leaders
  • Explore NYC with other Young Professionals

Program Topics

  • YP Leadership & Membership Advancement
  • Career Management & Security
  • Project Management, Entrepreneurship & Finances
  • AI-driven Solutions & Ethical Considerations
  • Climate Issues & Role of Young Professionals
  • Global Policies & Industry Engagement
