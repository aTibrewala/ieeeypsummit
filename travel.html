Where to Stay

We encourage attendees to book accommodations that best fit their preferences and budget. There is no official hotel block, and hotel accommodations are not included in Summit registration.

New York City and the surrounding metro area offer a wide range of hotel options with excellent public transit access to our venue at 60 Pine Street, NYC.

Many attendees choose to stay in New Jersey or outer boroughs of NYC to manage costs. The Summit schedule allows plenty of time for daily travel.

Recommended Areas with Good Transit Access

Jersey City, NJ

  • Fast PATH train access to World Trade Center (~5 min walk to venue)
  • Typically offers lower hotel rates than Manhattan
  • Example areas: Exchange Place, Newport

Hoboken, NJ

  • PATH access to World Trade Center or Midtown
